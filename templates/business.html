Methodology & Assumptions

Revenue at Risk = Actual Churners (185) × Avg Monthly Charges × Avg Lifetime months.
Campaign Cost = Retention Offer × All 282 targeted customers (not just churners — we don't know who will churn in advance).
Revenue Saved = Customers Successfully Retained × Avg Monthly Charges × Avg Lifetime. Retained = 185 churners × Success Rate.
These are illustrative assumptions. Real-world values would be validated with business and finance stakeholders before campaign launch.
